# Main Findings

## 1. Disease-associated multicellular community identified
AD lesions enriched for:
- MMP12+ dendritic cells
- mature migratory DCs (mmDC)
- cycling ILCs
- NK cells
- inflammatory CCL19+ IL4I1+ fibroblasts
- clonally expanded IL13+ IL22+ IL26+ T cells

Key idea:
Disease may be driven by a coordinated cellular community, not isolated cell types.

## 2. Altered keratinocyte differentiation / disrupted cornification
Important finding:
Cornified keratinocyte differentiation is abnormal in disease.

Interesting concept:
Immune cells may actively reshape epithelial differentiation.

Possible relevance:
